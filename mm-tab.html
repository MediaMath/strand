mm-tab

A single tab.

Example HTML Editor

<mm-tab tab-label="First" icon="info" active> <p>Is it that by its indefiniteness it shadows forth the heartless voids and immensities of the universe, and thus stabs us from behind with the thought of annihilation, when beholding the white depths of the milky way? Or is it, that as in essence whiteness is not so much a colour as the visible absence of colour; and at the same time the concrete of all colours; is it for these reasons that there is such a dumb blankness, full of meaning, in a wide landscape of snows&mdash;a colourless, all-colour of atheism from which we shrink? And when we consider that other theory of the natural philosophers, that all other earthly hues&mdash;every stately or lovely emblazoning&mdash;the sweet tinges of sunset skies and woods; yea, and the gilded velvets of butterflies, and the butterfly cheeks of young girls; all these are but subtile deceits, not actually inherent in substances, but only laid on from without; so that all deified Nature absolutely paints like the harlot, whose allurements cover nothing but the charnel-house within; and when we proceed further, and consider that the mystical cosmetic which produces every one of her hues, the great principle of light, for ever remains white or colourless in itself, and if operating without medium upon matter, would touch all objects, even tulips and roses, with its own blank tinge&mdash;pondering all this, the palsied universe lies before us a leper; and like wilful travellers in Lapland, who refuse to wear coloured and colouring glasses upon their eyes, so the wretched infidel gazes himself blind at the monumental white shroud that wraps all the prospect around him. And of all these things the Albino whale was the symbol. Wonder ye then at the fiery hunt?</p> </mm-tab>

attribute : active

loadExternal(urlcallback)
Lazily load this tab's content from an external html document, and attach a callback for when the tab content is loaded. The content of the external document must be surrounded by a <template> tag and may use Polymer's binding annotations. 		url : String

Relative path to an external html document to lazily load into the tab. callback : Function (optional)

Function to call when the tab is loaded. The template instance is passed as an argument to the callback, useful when accessing properties on the current binding scope is needed.
