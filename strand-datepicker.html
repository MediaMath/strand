strand-datepicker

A single and dual (range) date/time picker component including numeric and calendar based inputs

Example HTML Editor

<strand-button id="btn"> <label>Select a Date</label> </strand-button> <strand-datepicker id="dp" target="#btn" start-enabled end-enabled use-timezone="false" use-range="false"></strand-datepicker>
