mm-tabs

A fully-styled component for presenting tabbed content

Example HTML Editor

<style type="text/css"> .tab-content { box-sizing: border-box; padding: 15px; } </style> <mm-tabs tab-bar-offset="15"> <mm-tab tab-label="First" icon="info" active> <div class="tab-content"> <p>This is the first tab content.</p> </div> </mm-tab> <mm-tab tab-label="Second"> <div class="tab-content"> <p>This is the second tab content.</p> </div> </mm-tab> <mm-tab tab-label="Third"> <div class="tab-content"> <p>This is the third tab content.</p> </div> </mm-tab> </mm-tabs>
