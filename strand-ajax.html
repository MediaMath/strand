strand-ajax

A simple non rendered XMLHttpRequest wrapper api.

Example HTML Editor

<template is="dom-bind"> <strand-ajax method="GET" url="//strand.mediamath.com/LICENSE.txt" response="{{resp}}" response-type="text" auto> </strand-ajax> <strand-scroll-panel style="width:100%; height: 120px"> <strand-header size="tiny">data:</strand-header> <pre>{{resp}}</pre> </strand-scroll-panel> </template>
